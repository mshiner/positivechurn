Positive Churn

A Manifesto for Business Improvement



Having found that the fundamental rules still apply, even after 10 years, we thought we'd have a go at revisiting them. For the sake of good order they are
  1. You Can't Grow Your Way Out Of A Hole
  2. Cash Belongs In The Bank Not In The Warehouse
  3. Variable Labour Cost Does Not Exist
  4. Don't Let Your Overheads Grow Faster Than Your Business
  5. Cash IS King
  6. Everything Communicates.
